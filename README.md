#  **HR Analytics Report with Power BI**

##  **Overview**
This project focuses on analyzing HR data to uncover workforce trends and insights. The dashboard helps HR professionals monitor employee attrition, performance, demographics, and salary distribution to make data-driven decisions.

---

##  **Objectives**
- Analyze employee attrition rates by age, department, and job role  
- Visualize workforce demographics (gender, education, experience)  
- Explore performance ratings and satisfaction scores  
- Identify factors influencing employee turnover  

---

##  **Tools & Technologies**
- **Power BI**
- **DAX**
- **Excel / CSV Dataset**
- **Data Cleaning and Modeling**

---

##  **Key Insights**
- Most attrition occurs in early-career employees  
- R&D and Sales departments show higher turnover  
- Gender balance varies significantly across roles  
- Job satisfaction correlates strongly with salary growth
